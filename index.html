Our Story

Born in Syria. Forged through Egyptian creation. Sharpened with German precision. A brand built across borders — carrying truth in its threads.

The journey of ZEITU didn't begin in a fashion studio, but with a singular, raw moment of creation. The very first print—a conceptual manifesto. It represented the "Archive of Time" and the founding vision of the brand: to create something significant from scratch that remains static amidst the chaos of fashion

To transform a raw concept into a premium, engineered garment, ZEITU required a unique lens of precision. This evolution was led by a close friend and lead designer, a professional chef by trade. Bringing the meticulous discipline of the culinary arts—where the quality of every ingredient and the balance of every element is paramount—he translated the brand’s soul into the first hoodie designs.
